Our Beginnings

The Relief Story

2018

Foundation

Founded by a team of doctors and technologists to address healthcare accessibility challenges in urban and rural areas. Our vision was simple - quality healthcare for all.

2019

First Prototype

Launched our first telemedicine platform serving 5 clinics in West Bengal with basic consultation features. This humble beginning laid the foundation for our future growth.

2020

Pandemic Response

Scaled our services to support COVID-19 patients, reaching over 50,000 consultations in 6 months. Our platform became a lifeline for many during challenging times.

2022

National Expansion

Expanded to 12 states across India with partnerships with 200+ hospitals and 5000+ doctors. Our network now serves millions with comprehensive healthcare solutions.

2023

AI Integration

Implemented AI-driven symptom checker and predictive analytics for better diagnosis. Our technology now helps doctors make more accurate decisions faster.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Patient First

Every decision we make prioritizes patient well-being and positive health outcomes above all else. Their trust is our greatest responsibility.

Innovation

Constantly evolving our technology to deliver cutting-edge healthcare solutions that solve real problems for real people.

Compassion

Treating every patient with empathy, dignity, and personalized care. Healthcare is human, and we never forget that.

Integrity

Maintaining the highest ethical standards in all our practices and partnerships. We do what's right, not what's easy.

Collaboration

Working together with healthcare providers to deliver comprehensive care. We're stronger when we work as a team.

Accessibility

Making quality healthcare available to everyone, regardless of location or background. Healthcare is a right, not a privilege.
